Citizen Data Science Certificate

Tableau help lab

You see this courseon your dashboard because you enroll in one of the approved courses with Tableau assignment that might qualify you to earn a USF Muma College of Business Citizen Data Science Certificate. This program is available to business majors only. Why is this important? Through doing the Tableau projects, you will sharpen your skills in five of the ten top job skills identified by the World Economic Forum. Now more than ever, successful businesses depend on accurate, trusted data that empowers smarter decision-making in every corner of their organization.

The Tableau project from GEB 4890 must be one of the three that you submitted toward the certification. It is an opportunity for you to learn a skill that is valued by employers; getting valuable insights from data; skill to analyze big data to support important business decisions; critical thinking in solving problems. Each of the approved courses has a Tableau exercise where students analyze a dataset, produce visualizations dashboards and tell a story that emerges. Each of the assignments in these courses ends with a short video produced by the student, in which the student shows how Tableau can be used for a dataset in that course to generate important business insights (the most important aspect of learning Tableau). To earn a certificate, you must make your dashboard interactive and submit at least three different YouTube URL projects from three different courses to this Canvas course in addition to the course you are taking. This certification is not a requirement for graduation.
